About us

Sayed Design Energy Group, based in Kolkata, is a leading construction company led by Sabnam Yeasmin (B Tech) and Syed Ajmiruddin Ahammed (B. Tech. Civil Engg). We specialize in building work, PWD projects, railway construction, surveys, interiors, and soil testing, ensuring top-notch quality and innovation in every project.

Our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional results, with a portfolio that includes successful projects across various sectors. At Sayed Design Energy Group, we pride ourselves on building not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients through trust and excellence.
